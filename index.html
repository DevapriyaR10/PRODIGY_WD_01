    About Us

    Welcome to EcoGournet:

    Nourishing Connections, Minimizing Waste!

    At EcoGournet, we are passionate about forging meaningful connections within the food industry, households, and individuals who share a common goal: minimizing food wastage. Our mission revolves around fostering a community committed to prudent management, efficient exchange, and the optimal utilization of surplus edibles.

    Join the EcoGournet Community

    Whether you are a part of the food industry, a household, or an individual passionate about reducing food waste, EcoGournet welcomes you to join our community. Together, we can make a significant impact on the way we approach and manage our food resources. Join us on this exciting journey towards a more sustainable and flavorful future. At EcoGournet, every connection, every effort, and every meal matters. Let's create a world where we waste less and savor more!

    Features

    Inventory Management

    EcoGournet keeps track of expiry dates and sends timely reminders to consume or donate items before they go to waste.

    Donation

    EcoGournet has the contacts of all nearby food banks shelters and charitable organizations to donate extra food.

    Tracking Usage

    EcoGournet AI analyzes the patterns of food usage and gives suggestions regarding to prevent overstocking

    Recipe Suggestions

    EcoGournet AI will suggest recipes to the users using the items available in the inventory.

    History and Records

    EcoGournet will keep track of users food saving history and their progress.

    Food Content

    Read articles, blogs and books regarding food saving and its impact on the environment as well as on lives of others.
